Privacy Policy

Fish Cannon: Deep Sea Merge (com.aispeeds.fishshooter) · Last updated: 2026-07-22

Fish Cannon: Deep Sea Merge ("the app") is a casual game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we do NOT collect

The app has no account system, no registration, and no server of its own. Your game progress (scores, coins, fish codex, settings) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded by us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The mobile app shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data to serve and measure ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier / IDFA, where you permit it)
  • Advertising interaction data and app usage data (e.g. ad views, taps)
  • Coarse location (derived from IP address)
  • Diagnostics (crash and performance data)

See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from AdMob.

Your choices

  • iOS App Tracking Transparency: on first launch you can allow or deny tracking. If you deny, ads are non-personalized.
  • EU/UK/Swiss users (GDPR): a consent dialog lets you accept, manage, or refuse personalized ads. You can change your choice anytime from the consent options.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings at any time.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
