Privacy Policy — Dungeon Sweep

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Dungeon Sweep ("the game") is developed by Zhongyuan Tang. This policy explains what data the game handles, on the web (mines.ai-speeds.com) and in the iOS/Android apps.

No account, no personal data collected by us

The game has no sign-up and no login. We do not collect, store, or transmit your name, email, or any personal information to our servers. Game progress (runs, badges, settings) is saved locally on your device only.

Advertising (mobile apps only)

The mobile apps show optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID / IDFA), coarse location, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data in AdMob.

  • iOS: the app asks for tracking permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, ads are still shown but are not personalized using your identifier.
  • EU/UK/CH users: a consent form (Google UMP) is shown before personalized ads; you can change your choice anytime from the game's settings.

The web version at mines.ai-speeds.com shows no ads and uses no analytics or tracking cookies.

Your rights

Depending on your region (GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws), you may have rights to access or delete data held by ad providers. Ad data is processed by Google; see the links above. Local game data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing browser storage.

Children

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are served through Google AdMob under its family-safety policies.
